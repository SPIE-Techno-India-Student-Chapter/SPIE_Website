ABOUT US

The Techno India SPIE Student Chapter, founded in the year 2014 by the then alumni of the Electronics and instrumentation engineering department,has been the sole organisation of the college which hails its parent organisation as an international acclaimed one.The International society of optics and photonics (SPIE), located in the industrial hub of San Diego, CA,USA funds our organization for the noble deeds year round which includes organizing outreach programs and lectures of some eminent personalities in the field of science and technology. The chapter boasts of encouraging the budding scientists in the students to do something for the greater good.

Our objectives are:

    <<<<<<< HEAD
  • To educate the masses about the pivotal role played by light and how it can be utilised to usher sustainable development in the society.
  • To conduct successful outreach activities aimed at students belonging to marginalized sections of society (e.g. tribals, orphans) of rural India
  • To teach basic science to underprivileged school students by means of interactive demonstrations.
  • To prepare optics and renewable energy based models capable of improving the quality of life.
  • To carry out research in optics, photonics and other related disciplines.
  • To provide a chance for our students to learn and interact with renowned scientists from around the world by organizing guest lectures
  • To establish a platform for students seeking to pursue higher studies in science and technology.
